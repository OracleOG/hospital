# Hospital Website Design Plan

This plan is the blueprint for the site build. It includes my proposed design direction, page structure, and the exact info I will need from you to complete the website.

---

## 1) Design Direction (Updated For Professional, Neat, Clean)

**Concept name:** Clinical Trust

**Visual vibe:** Highly professional, minimal, conversion-focused. Crisp layout, ample whitespace, medical clarity, and high trust.

**Typography:**
- Display font: "Fraunces" (for headings; trustworthy, humane)
- Body font: "Source Sans 3" (for readability and long-form content)

**Color system (clean + trustworthy):**
- Primary: Deep Teal `#0F4C5C`
- Secondary: Soft Mint `#DFF2E1`
- Accent: Warm Coral `#F26B5B`
- Neutral: Mist White `#F7F9FA`
- Text: Charcoal `#1F2A30`

**Visual elements:**
- Clean grid, large section padding
- Rounded cards with soft shadow (very subtle)
- Light gradient or soft wave pattern in hero background
- Photo overlays with soft tint for readability

**Motion:**
- Smooth fade/slide on load for hero and key sections
- Staggered reveal for cards and stats

**Conversion emphasis:**
- Strong primary CTA above the fold
- Sticky "Book Appointment" button on mobile
- Trust markers near the hero (24/7 hours, on-site lab, on-site imaging)

If you want a different style (luxury, academic, pediatric, community clinic, etc.), tell me and I will revise.
